# Product Brief: Jawed Habib Salon Digital Twin

## 1. Executive Summary
Development of an intelligent 'Digital Twin' system for **Jawed Habib Hair & Beauty Salon, Kurnool**. The system will act as the central operational brain, managing dual-gender resource scheduling, client relations, inventory, and staff performance to maximize throughput and premium service delivery.

## 2. Business Goals
- **Maximize Resource Utilization**: Ensure zero downtime for the 19 active workstations.
- **Premium Experience**: Provide seamless booking and personalized service tracking for high-end clients.
- **Operational Efficiency**: Automate inventory reordering, staff shifts, and shift handovers.
- **Franchise Compliance**: Track metrics and usage patterns required by the Jawed Habib brand.

## 3. Key Resource Constraints
The digital twin must strictly enforce capacity limits for the following infrastructure:

| Area | Women | Men | Total | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Cutting/Styling** | 4 Chairs | 6 Chairs | 10 | High throughput zone |
| **Washing** | 1 Bay | 1 Bay | 2 | Critical Bottleneck: Strict scheduling required |
| **Facials/Treatments** | 2 Rooms | 2 Rooms | 4 | Long duration, private service |
| **Pedi/Mani** | 2 Stations| 1 Station | 3 | Specialized equipment |

## 4. User Personas
- **Client**: Wants easy booking, no waiting, remembers their preferences.
- **Stylist/Technician**: Wants clear schedule, access to client history, automated inventory requests.
- **Manager/Owner**: Real-time dashboard of revenue, occupancy rates, and staff performance.
- **Digital Twin (The Agent)**: Proactive management of conflicts, inventory alerts, and marketing suggestions.

## 5. Assumptions & Defaults
- **Operating Hours**: 9:00 AM - 9:00 PM (Daily, 12hr window).
- **Staff**: 8-10 Employees assumed based on station count.
- **Platform**: Web-based dashboard (Manager) + Automated Booking (Client via Web/WhatsApp).
